Leon turns himself invisible for 6 seconds, allowing him to either retreat or ambush an enemy. A unique visual and sound effect plays when Leon goes invisible, even if he is in a bush. Enemy Brawlers can see him if he is within 4 tiles of them. If Leon attacks while invisible, he loses his invisibility. If he takes damage during his Super, he will momentarily be visible.

Leon is a Legendary Brawler who has moderate health and a high damage output at close range. He attacks by flicking out four Spinner Blades that sweep and deal less damage further away.
